Highlights of Summer 2023

This summer really felt like a long one. After taking one year off of school and working all of fall and winter, I wanted to enjoy my time before I jumped back into academics.

Table of Contents

  1. Camping Trip
  2. Celebrating my birthday
  3. Trip to New York City
  4. Bonus: Cats!

Camping Trip

This summer I went camping for the first time with friends instead of family at Fitzroy Provincial Park. I wasn't sure if I would be built for the outdoors - I still don't think I am - but I had a lot of fun. We made hot dogs and hamburgers, roasted marshmallows and swam in the lake.

Celebrating my birthday

I normally don't do much for my birthday - but since I turned 19 this summer, I wanted to go out and have fun. First, my boyfriend and I went to Izakaya Shingen, a Japanese restaurant where I had a drink and some onigiri. Then, I was treated to some dessert at Amore Gelato. Lastly, we went to a karaoke bar where Jackson and I performed Barbie Girl by Aqua in honour of the Barbie Movie being released later that month.

Trip to New York City

I visit New York City nearly every year, however, it never gets old. I stayed with my friend in their apartment and they showed me a ton of cool places. We went to a rooftop patio in Jersey City with an amazing view of the NYC skyline. We visited the Nintendo store in Rockefeller Center. I bought a tiny plushie of Volcarona, one of my favourite Pokémon. We also went to a claw machine café and it was so cool even though I didn't win anything. I can't wait to visit next year, hopefully.
